CrowdSec

Jochim is a security-conscious individual who is looking for an effective way to protect his servers and systems from a range of security threats. After doing some research, he has decided to use CrowdSec as his primary security solution.

CrowdSec

Jochim is impressed by the features offered by CrowdSec, including its ability to automatically blacklist malicious IPs and the option to customize response actions based on specific threat scenarios. He appreciates the flexibility that CrowdSec provides, with its ability to integrate with various tools and technologies, and the ease of use that allows him to get up and running quickly.

Google Cloud

Jochim makes Google Cloud firewalls by first accessing the Google Cloud console, then navigating to the Networking section and selecting the Firewalls option. From there, he defines the firewall rules by specifying the source and destination IP addresses, protocols, ports, and actions. Jochim ensures that the firewalls are configured to allow only the necessary traffic and denies all other unauthorized access attempts. Finally, he tests the firewall rules to verify that they are working as intended and providing optimal security for his Google Cloud environment.

Bitninja

Jochim makes use of BitNinja for cyber attack prevention by installing the software on his server. BitNinja automatically scans and blocks suspicious traffic and malicious IP addresses, as well as brute-force attacks, DDoS attacks, and other types of cyber threats. Jochim can configure the software to meet his specific security needs, and he receives real-time notifications of any blocked attacks, so he can quickly respond to any threats. With BitNinja, Jochim can proactively protect his server from cyber attacks, ensuring the safety and security of his data and users.
